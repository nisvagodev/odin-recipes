Spanish Rice Chicken

Colombian or Venezuelan style rice

This recipe is so quick and is so very simple! It is perfect for making in a recreational vehicle (RV) galley for a quick, delicious dinner on the road and on the go! Andale, pues!

Ingridients

  • 1 Homemade chicken
  • 2 purple onions
  • 2 white onions
  • 1 diced bell bell pepper
  • 2 teaspoon achiote oil
  • 2 teaspoon of herbite
  • 1 carrot cut into pieces
  • 1 teaspoon garlic paste
  • ½ cup cooked peas
  • ½ cup raisins
  • Salt, pepper and cumin

Steps

  1. In a pot of boiling water, cook the chicken in pieces, seasoned with salt, pepper and cumin. When it is cooked, remove it from the pot.
  2. Prepare the rice using the sifted water used to cook the chicken.
  3. Separately in a frying pan fry in oil-achiote, red onion, white onion, bell bell pepper, season with salt, pepper, cumin, then add the cooked carrot and peas, shredded chicken, raisins and cook for about 5 minutes.
