Pancake with berries and honey

American style fluffy hot cakes

These pancakes are the fluffiest in existence and can be prepared in a matter of minutes.

Ingridients

  • 150 grams of wheat flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon standard sugar
  • 225 milliliters of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 piece of melted butter

Steps

  1. Sift together the flour, salt, baking powder and sugar. Form a well in the center and pour in the milk, then add the eggs and melted butter. Beat well until you have a lump-free dough.
  2. Heat a comal or skillet over medium heat and grease with butter. To make sure the pan is hot enough, test with a drop of batter. Pour portions of the batter over the hot butter.
  3. Cook each hot cake until bubbles begin to form on the surface and the edges look dry. Flip and cook on the other side for 1 to 2 minutes, until lightly browned.
  4. Serve with butter, fruit and bee, maple or agave honey.
